Ferid Murad
Ferid Murad

Freddie Murad, an Albanian-American doctor and pharmacologist. Obtained 1998 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine, honorary member of Kosovo Academy of Science and Arts. Foreign academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

George Fitzgerald Smoot III

George Smoot, a professor of physics at the University of California, Berkeley, astrophysicist and cosmologist. The 2006 Nobel Prize in Physics was shared by George Smoot and John Matherin for "Discovering the Black Form and Anisotropy of Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation." This work using COBE satellites helps to consolidate the theory of the universal thunderbolt.

Erwin Neher

German biophysicist. In 1991, he won the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine. He is now the director of the Max Planck Institute for Biophysical Chemistry and a professor at the University of Göttingen.

Dan Shechtman

Israeli material scientist. Sheikh Tuttman was born in Tel Aviv, after obtaining a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering at the Israel Institute of Technology, she successively obtained Master of Materials Science and Ph.D. He is Phillips Tobias, Professor of Materials Science, Israel Institute of Technology, Assistant Ames Laboratory, US Department of Energy, and Materials Science Professor, Iowa State University.
