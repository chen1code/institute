  • Nanjing Science Research Institute of the joint innovation with Ferid Murad, George Smoot, Randy, Erwin Neil schekman and Dan Shekhtman five Nobel laureates as the core of the expert advisory team, has a master's degree and above the core R & D personnel more than twenty people, mostly from domestic famous university institutes, have important academic influence at home and abroad.

  • Through deep learning, image processing, pattern recognition and other traditional science and technology disciplines, to explore and develop the application of artificial intelligence in different industries, medical, security, finance and other fields, for the construction and promotion of intelligent ecology to provide advanced Data Processing and Decision Support Technology, Products and Services.
