Smart Registered Robot
Application of advanced learning, natural language processing and other in-line cutting-edge technology to create intelligent registration robot to reduce the pressure of triage hospitals to fully improve the convenience of patient visits.

This section is divided into registered robot linked to the registration screen and voice registered two major functional areas, including the three major

  • voice registered
  • Site search
  • Knowledge map
Voice registration

In the process of using, patients do not need to manually input any information, only need to describe the specific condition of the robot, the robot can quickly link to the background knowledge map, according to the keywords in the descriptive text to give the recommended department and register for the patient. This function can reduce the burden on triage staff and registration windows and provide accurate triage for patients to avoid triage mistakes caused by physical discomfort, fatigue or other reasons of triage nurses.

Site search

In the touch screen registration function partition, set up by clicking the site of discomfort, the symptoms of direct registration to the department's functions, the patient pre-treatment work flow, rationalization. Set the word function mainly to consider the patient's own disease and departmental judgment ability differences, colleagues can also greatly improve the accuracy of triage.

Knowledge map

The knowledge map can strongly guarantee the accuracy of this guide robot. By inputting the symptoms, diseases and corresponding links, it can accurately and quickly output the corresponding department no matter what method the patient chooses. The accuracy is up to 90%. Not only that, there is an active learning process in the knowledge map, which not only can discover new words constantly in the course of named entity recognition, but also can make the knowledge map self-perfected and improve the accuracy of the robot's triage.
