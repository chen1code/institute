Medical

Founded in 2017 as a Wholly Foreign Owned Enterprise of Nobel Laureate Ferid Murad in China, the company is based in the field of artificial intelligence with advanced deep learning and natural language processing technologies to provide smart medical services provide the solution.

Enterprise is located in Jiangning District, Nanjing, Jiangning Economic and Technological Development Zone has been the support of the management committee, formed a mature core R & D team. In addition, the company is also actively cooperating with major universities in Nanjing to jointly establish a school-enterprise cooperation project, combining smart medical and gene sequencing to promote the further development of precision treatment technology.

Founded in 2017, Nanjing Lingshen Information Technology Co., Ltd. is a Wholly Foreign Owned Enterprise of Nobel Laureate George Fitzgerald Smoot in China engaged in quantum cryptography and dynamic image recognition projects.

For Motion Picture Recognition projects, the company provides solutions for Artificial Intelligence security based on leading-edge image processing and pattern recognition technologies.

The company is located in Nanjing Jiangning Development Zone, Jiangning Economic and Technological Development Zone has received the support of the Management Committee, Nobel Laureate George Smoot served as technical adviser, led by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a number of data scientists and engineers set up a mature Technology research and development and implementation of the team, with accurate face recognition, vehicle identification and other technologies for the school, finance, property and other industries to provide users with comprehensive technical support and improve the solution.

Institute of corporate management committed to building a learning organization, to maintain the vitality of the enterprise life.

First, by taking full advantage of the features of modern computer technology and the Internet, deep teams of senior data scientists are able to build multiple feedbacks and open learning systems in the process of research and development, mobilizing the company's learning and sharing atmosphere, such as weekly Knowledge sharing experience, team members to achieve cooperative learning and group intelligence development, research and development activities to provide a steady stream of inspiration and direction.

Second, the company's internal implementation of flat management from the top project manager to the bottom of the project implementation staff, separated by very few levels, to the greatest extent possible to move the decision-making authority to R & D personnel, so that researchers have the core technology has sufficient Of the autonomy, and be responsible for the results produced.

Third, the company internal implementation of "self-management" by the team members to find their own problems, analyze the causes and to develop appropriate policies, their own inspection results, their own assessment summary, give full play to the enthusiasm of team members, inspire good ideas and experiences Collision.

From a functional point of view, the company focuses on both R & D and product development. The financial and HR resources are tailored by a professional financial team and a human resources team to develop a sound financial system and human resources system for the company. The company's main staff is divided into research and development and product departments, as much as possible to streamline the structure, the human resources will be focused on the company's main business, enhance team efficiency and the company's rapid response capabilities, effectively avoid the risk of research and development.
